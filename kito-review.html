The ketogenic diet is scientifically proven for weight loss, but here's the reality: most people quit within the first month. Why? Because keto meal planning is complicated, time-consuming, and confusing.

Tracking macros, calculating net carbs, finding recipes that fit your ratios, creating shopping lists, and meal prepping — it can take hours every week. No wonder so many people give up before seeing results.
