Frequently Asked Questions

Does Java Burn change the taste of my coffee?
No. Java Burn is specifically formulated to be tasteless and odorless. It dissolves quickly in hot or cold beverages without altering the flavor, aroma, or texture of your coffee.
When is the best time to take it?
Morning is ideal as it's designed to work with your coffee. The natural ingredients may help support energy levels throughout the day. Avoid taking late in the afternoon if you're sensitive to caffeine.
How long should I use it to see results?
Individual results vary significantly. Some people report feeling more energetic within days, while metabolic changes typically require consistent use over 8-12 weeks combined with diet and exercise. There is no guaranteed timeline for results.
Can I use it with other beverages?
While formulated for coffee, the powder can technically be added to other beverages. However, the ingredients are specifically selected to work synergistically with coffee's natural compounds for optimal potential benefit.
